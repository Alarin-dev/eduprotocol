Edu protocol

Learn by Teaching , Teach by Learning


What is Edu protocol?

-A value-based learning platform
-Teach a skill then Earn a token
-Use the token to learn another skill

How it works (3 steps)

[Teach]
Share what you know

[Earn Tokens]
Get rewarded for teaching

[Learn]
Use tokens to learn new skills

Who is it for?

✔️ Beginners
✔️ Self-learners
✔️ People who want real experience
