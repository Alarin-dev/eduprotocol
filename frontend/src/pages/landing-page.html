What is Edu Protocol? you may ask...

it's a value based learning platform!. it works by you teaching a skill you have to others to earn tokens!. to which then you will use that token to learn a skill from another user.

How does it work? (three steps!)

[Teach] : Share what you know
[Earn tokens] : get rewarded for teaching
[Learn] :Use tokens to learn new skills.

Who is it for? does it suits me?

It's for:
✔️Beginners
✔️Self-learners
✔️people who wants real experience
